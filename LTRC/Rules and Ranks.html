Welcome to the explanation of LTRC: Limitless Team Racing Competition. In these series of events you try to get your team as many Accolades as you possibly can, through team victories, but also your own. Every week, events will be organised in which either you and your friends, or just you, can compete to make your team (and yourself) the #1. The ultimate goal here is to make your team the best. Accolades are rewarded on performances in these events, or taken away.
4
Let's explain the formats and rules. Firstly, there are 6 types of events that can be played: FFA, 2v2, 3v3, 4v4, Wars and 200cc. These formats will be cycled through and voted upon prior to the events. For team events, getting Accolades is pretty simple. Accolades are rewarded for performing well. With most seedings, that means being rewarded accolades for being in the top half, and accolades being taken away for being in the lower half.
5
An addition to the events is called The Requirement. In these events you will be given a certain task to complete for an additional amount of Accolades. Failure to do said task however, means an additional loss in Accolades as well. Tasks will include a placement requirement for you or your team, or a total point requirement for you or your team. Extra MMR (1.33x) is gained for high gambles.
6
Teams can be of a maximum of 6 players (5 players and a potential sub/coach). These 6 players can all sign up for events (though depending on the format, not all of you may be allowed to play, so keep that in mind). Accolades you earn add up to the team total. Accolades you have earned for a team are not given to the new team, however.
